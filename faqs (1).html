🍽️ What types of cuisine does Ottawa Kabab serve?
Ottawa Kabab serves Asian cuisine, especially Indian dishes. Our menu includes both meat and vegetarian options: appetizers, dips, salads, entrees, and desserts.
📦 Does Ottawa Kabab offer takeout and delivery?
Yes! We offer takeout and delivery. Call us for pickup orders, or use UberEats and SkipTheDishes for home delivery.
🥗 Are there vegetarian options available?
Absolutely. Try our hummus, baba ganoush, muhammara, or vegetarian platter with falafel, grape leaves, and tabbouleh.
🎉 Do you offer catering services?
Yes! We cater for weddings, corporate events, and private parties. Vegetarian and meat options available. Customizable menus too.
🌟 What are your most popular dishes?
Our bestsellers include fatteh hummus with almonds & pomegranate, grilled meat/chicken cubes, smoky baba ganoush, and mutabal.
🕐 What are your hours of operation?
We are open daily from 11 AM to 9 PM (except Thursdays). During Ramadan: 12 PM to 10 PM.
