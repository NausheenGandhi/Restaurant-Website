It's Simple. We Serve the Best Food.

  • We take pride in serving the finest Asian cuisine, featuring homemade and authentic dishes from India, Korea, and China. Whether you're craving sizzling Indian kabab, a hearty Korean breakfast, or tabbouleh the Chinese way, our menu has something for everyone.

  • One of our specialties is our hearty Korean breakfast, which includes dishes like bibimbap bowls loaded with fresh green veggies, pomegranate seeds, chickpeas, and crispy egg. Whether you're a meat-eater or a vegetarian, you'll find something to love on our breakfast menu.

  • We also offer a wide range of kebab plates, including chicken, all grilled to perfection over charcoal. Our dips and sauces are amazing in both quantity and quality and are the perfect complement to any meal. For dessert, enjoy homemade pastries and sweets like almond-topped semolina cakes (halwa) and sweet dumplings filled with cheese and cream.

  • At MN's Cuisine, we strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere where our customers can enjoy delicious food and experience the rich culture of Asia. Whether you're joining us for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, we look forward to sharing our passion for food with you.
